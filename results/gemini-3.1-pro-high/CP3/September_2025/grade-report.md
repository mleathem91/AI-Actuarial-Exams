## Persona Insights

### Strengths
- The AI excels at adapting its tone and vocabulary to the target audience. It correctly identified complex actuarial concepts (mortality tables, algebraic formulas) and stripped them out without losing the core business meaning.
- Section structuring and signposting were pristine, resulting in a highly readable and logical paper.
- The reflective questions (Q2) were answered methodically and accurately matched the marking scheme's priorities.

### Weaknesses identified by the Harsh Marker
- The AI chose a simplistic 5-year sampling interval for its data tables. In doing so, it completely missed the age 76 outlier data point (an 8.4% jump in Option 2) which was the central piece of evidence supporting the core narrative about "erratic increases". This indicates a lack of deeper exploratory data analysis prior to generating the communication piece.
- Inclusion of 2 decimal places in the tables was deemed superfluous accuracy for an audience trying to grasp high-level conceptual changes.

### Benefit of the Doubt given by the Generous Marker
- The Generous marker accepted the 5-year interval table as a deliberate "summarisation" technique suitable for board-level trustees, rather than a failure to plot the exact turning points of the underlying data.
- The Generous marker accepted tables as fully equivalent to charts for the "visual aid" requirement.
